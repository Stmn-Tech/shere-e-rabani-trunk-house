About Us

Established before 2000, Shere-e-Rabani Trunk House is a family-run business led by Mr. Tahir Maqsood Nasir and his sons Salman, Zaman, Nouman, and Zeeshan. We specialize in steel and metal products including safes, trunks, mutti, tubs, barola, steel pillars, ducts, and more. Our expertise extends to electric, gas, and steel welding services. We combine traditional craftsmanship with modern technology to deliver durable and reliable products to our customers across Punjab, Pakistan.
